Interview Questions & Answers

1. What are your favorite hobbies?

Video games – especially horror games like the Resident Evil series and first-person shooters such as Halo and Call of Duty.

Traveling – including a major road trip from Florida → Colorado → New Jersey → Colorado → Florida.

After moving from New Jersey to Florida, Adam later relocated to Colorado for a few years so his son could finish high school. He enjoyed traveling along the coasts during that time.

2. What is your most and least favorite food and why?

Favorite: Pizza — something he misses living in Florida, especially New Jersey–style pizza. Fortunately, a local pizza shop owned by a New Jersey native gives him a weekly fix.

Least favorite: Eggplant parmesan — “just about the worst food on the planet.”

3. What is your biggest accomplishment?

Being a single father and raising his son, Derek.

4. What is your deepest regret?

Not saving money sooner — spending too much when it could have been saved, and not having it when it was needed most.

5. What is something you are most proud of?

His son Derek joining the army.

6. What is something you would like to forget?

Being hit by a car in 2005, which caused a disabling back injury. He was pushing Derek in a stroller at the time — thankfully, Adam took all the impact and his son was unharmed.

7. What are you most looking forward to in 2026?

His sisters’ weddings — Katie in March 2026 and Megan in October 2026. One wedding down, one to go.

8. What is your most prized possession?

His massive DVD collection — filled with his favorite movies and still growing.

9. What are your top three favorite movies?

Drop Dead Fred, Stepbrothers, and Masters of the Universe (1987).

10. If you could have lunch with anyone alive or dead, who would it be and why?

Jensen Ackles and Jared Padalecki from Supernatural — because they seem like genuinely great people, and he would love to sit down and have a good conversation with them.
